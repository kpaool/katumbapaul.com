About Katumba Jonathan Paul

I studied at Namilyango Junior Boys for primary school and then continued to Namilyango College for my high school. While at Namilyango College, I taught myself a lot of software development together with innovation to develop a number of projects with the biggest project being an artificial swamp that was helping villages in Namilyango to get clean water. This project won the National Water and Sewerage corporation innovation challenge as the best water protection project. Besides that, I worked on a number of other projects such as online voting, sports, and health tracking system.

Currently serving as the CEO at Minute5 , an online grocery delivery service for fresh food. I'm a tech enthusiast and always developing and learning new emerging technologies that I can put my hands on.

I'm also a MEST EIT (Entreprenuer In Training) which is an oppportunity to meet brilliant people to work with on solutions to solve the big African problems.
